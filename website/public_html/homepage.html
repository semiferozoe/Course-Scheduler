About Us

This website and its tools were created by students, for students. Our goal is to help students achieve greatness by easing the process and stressfulness of selecting classes each semester. Traditionally, courses are difficult to find and line-up for the future. Now, it will be easy to know which classes you need to take, when to take them, and how close you are to graduating!
